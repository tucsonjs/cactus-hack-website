Cactus Hack?

Cactus Hack is a free, one day web development event that focuses on cool projects and community. You and your team (team construction is during the event) will get an afternoon to build the most awesome, mega feature-packed, Tucson-themed web app possible. When time is up, everyone shows off their goods and we give out some awesome prizes.

All skillsets are welcome, from veteran designers and developers, to those just getting started with web development. There is literally no reason for your nerdy butt to sit at home. So come down, meet some sweet people, and have a fun time building something amazing. But don't take our word for it, see what other people are saying about their excitement for the event.
